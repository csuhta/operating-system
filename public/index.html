Operating System

What do you do when you’ve hit your storage limit?

Can Google tell when you're happy? Joseph Price tears open his online data to define his quantified self in this strange, intimate, analytics love story.

Running time: 55 minutes, no intermission

About the Show

Joseph Price, writer, performer

Joseph Price is a playwright, storyteller, and technologist. He has told stories on stage with The Stoop, Story League, Mortified, and SpeakeasyDC, where he is also a teacher.

Other Capital Fringe credits include This Digital Life: Basic Instructions for Coping with the 21st Century (2007, playwright), The Sin Show (2009, performer), Logic, Luck and Love (co-director), and e-Geaux (beta) (Capital Fringe Festival 2011) which was featured on NPR's All Things Considered.

Amy Couchoud, director

Amy Couchoud is a director, teaching artist and producer who has been involved in the Washington, DC performing arts community for almost 15 years. She is currently a faculty member at SpekeasyDC and an associate of Pinky Swear Productions.

Other Capital Fringe credits include Chocolate Jesus and Chocolate Jesus: The Resurrection (director), The Sin Show (co-director), Logic, Luck and Love (co-director), and e-Geaux (beta).
